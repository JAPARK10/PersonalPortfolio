  • Hackathon from a private company - Minship
    This Weekend was one of those with little sleep and a lot of brainpower. The Hackathon began on Friday afternoon with a little welcoming and detailed briefing. We then were divided into groups of three and tasked with coming up with a solution to help the company prevent man-in-the-middle attacks when receiving and paying invoices from their service providers. Our solution was to create a verified list of service providers with their bank details. So only when a service provider sends an invoice for the first time or when the service providers bank details change an extended verification of the new details using google authenticator is required. Having a well-designed and working solution implemented by the end of the weekend, earned our group a decent reward and a job offer.

    Used technologies: Git, Github, Google authenticator, Java, python, postgreSQL
